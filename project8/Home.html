Welcome to Leahy's Bees

Leahy's Bees is a small backyard beekeeping operation that focuses on healthy hives, clean honey, and gentle handling. Every jar of honey that leaves our shop comes from bees that are cared for through the full season, from the first spring blossoms to the last fall flowers.

Our goal is to help people understand how important bees are to everyday life while offering honey and wax products that taste as good as they look. On this site you can explore some of our favorite photos, read short tributes to standout hives, and learn more about what goes into each frame of honey.
